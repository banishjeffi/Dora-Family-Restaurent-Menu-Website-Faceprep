The Dora Legacy

Located in the heart of Karungal, Kanyakumari, Dora Family Restaurant is a local landmark where metropolitan sophistication meets the soul of tradition. We set out to redefine the local dining landscape, offering a "metro-style" experience that stays true to the rich, authentic flavors of our heritage.

Our journey is built on a foundation of culinary excellence and genuine hospitality. From our legendary charcoal-fired tandoori grills to the sweet, handcrafted perfection of our signature Mango Falooda and Chocolate Sharjah, we believe in creating more than just meals—we create memories. Whether you're here for an intimate brunch or a grand family milestone, our space is designed to make you feel right at home.
