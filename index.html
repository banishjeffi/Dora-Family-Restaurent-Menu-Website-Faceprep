Our Story

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Karungal, Kanyakumari, Dora Family Restaurant has redefined the local dining experience. We bridge the gap between "metro-style" sophistication and the warm, welcoming spirit of a family home.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to serve incredible food in a space that feels alive. Today, we are celebrated for our diverse menu—ranging from smoky charcoal grills to artisan desserts like our signature Mango Falooda. Whether you're here for a quick brunch or a grand celebration, we invite you to taste the passion in every bite.
